sleptontools ("we," "us," "this site") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you visit sleptontools.com, how we use it, and what choices you have. Using the site means you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect automatically

Like most websites, we collect some information automatically through cookies and similar technologies:

  • Usage data: pages viewed, time on site, referring site, browser type, and approximate location (city/country level, derived from IP address). We may use Google Analytics or a similar service for this.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: when you click a link to a partner site (YNAB, Rocket Money, Acorns, Webull, Empower, or others), a tracking cookie is set by our affiliate network (Impact.com) or the partner directly, so that partner knows the referral came from us if you sign up. This is how we earn a commission — it does not cost you anything extra, and we do not see your account details or activity on the partner's site.

Information you provide directly

If you sign up for our email list (such as the 5-Minute Money Audit or any future newsletter), we collect the email address you provide. We use it only to send the content you signed up for and occasional related updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link in that email, or by contacting us directly.

Affiliate disclosure

sleptontools participates in affiliate marketing programs, meaning we may earn a commission on qualifying purchases or signups made through links on this site, at no extra cost to you. This is disclosed on every page containing affiliate links. Our editorial opinions, scores, and recommendations are formed independently of any affiliate relationship — a partner paying us does not influence whether or how we recommend their product.

Third-party links and services

This site links to third-party websites (app providers, our affiliate network, our email provider, and our own Gumroad store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies directly.

Third parties whose tools may be active on this site: Impact.com (affiliate tracking), Google (Search Console, and Analytics if enabled), Netlify (hosting), and an email service provider (once connected) for newsletter delivery.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for the purposes described above. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings — doing so may affect how some parts of the site function, but the site will remain readable without them.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your choices

  • You can browse this site without providing any personal information.
  • You can decline or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • You can unsubscribe from any email list at any time.
  • You can contact us to ask what information, if any, we hold about you.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves — for example, when we add an email provider or analytics tool. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.
